The Big Bang

The Big Bang is the very starting point of the universe! At this point, we do not know what happened natural laws begin to break down. We may never know, but we do know that the universe as we know it started here!

The Expansion of The Universe

Einestien theory of relativity along with edwin hubble discovery proved the expansion of the universe. So what does that means! From a hot dens elementary particles the universe expands to form evrey thing around us!!!

Matter

Within just one second, the universe had expanded and cooled enough for the first element to form the hydrogen atom!

The Birth of Light

As time passed and the universe continued expanding, hydrogen atoms began to clump together. After millions of years, gravity compressed them until at last stars were born.

Death of the First Generation of Stars

Even stars come to an end but the death of the first generation of stars was truly unique. Their explosive ends scattered the elements that formed all the terrestrial objects we know today. Thanks to them, our solar system exists, and we have a home called Earth. These deaths also triggered the formation of the next generation of stars.
